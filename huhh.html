Creepy Crawly Lino Prints?!

Vincent Robertson

August '24

In August, I started using some leftover lino print equipment from a course my mum had completed a while ago. Once I inevitably ran out of this equipment, I decided to experiment with creating digital prints that had the same, lino-y, inky effect. Using custom downloadable brushes I found on Pinterest, I created these bug-themed prints that look a bit like physical lino prints, with black, red, and white colour themes. The technique hasn't quite been perfected yet, but I hope I can use these samples as a starting point.
